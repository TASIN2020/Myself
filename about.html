About Me


Md. Tasin Tazwar was born in January02, 1998 in Dhaka, Bangladesh. He has a sister who is currently a university student. He has grown up in a middle class family. His father is a businessman and his mother is a teacher. From his school life he has become a good student. Besides his study he was associated with co-curricular activities in his school life. He used to do debates, do singing, making projects for science fair. He achieved more than 50 certificates from his school life through co-curricular activities. He had an extraordinary ability to lead a club as well. He was a General Secretary of Gregorian Debating Club . After Completing his school life he got chance to read in Notre Dame College which is one of the best colleges in Bangladesh. He is always good in analytical abilities. He starts his Bachelor studies in Computer Science in Brac University. Currently he is in final year and associated with IEEE Brac University Student Branch. Recently Computer Science and Engineering department of Brac University has recruited him as a Teaching Assistant for his extraordinary academic results.
